## üîß WORKFLOW DETERMINATION LOGIC

### Rules for Automatic Workflow Routing:

1. **If `directorate` is NULL or empty:**
   - Unit reports to Chief Director
   - Workflow: `EMPLOYEE ‚Üí SUPERVISOR ‚Üí UNIT_HEAD ‚Üí CHIEF_DIRECTOR ‚Üí HR_OFFICER`

2. **If `directorate` = "Finance & Administration":**
   - Standard workflow: `EMPLOYEE ‚Üí SUPERVISOR ‚Üí UNIT_HEAD ‚Üí DIRECTOR ‚Üí HR_OFFICER`
   - **Exception**: If `unit` = "Human Resource Management Unit (HRMU)":
     - Special workflow: `EMPLOYEE ‚Üí SUPERVISOR ‚Üí UNIT_HEAD ‚Üí DIRECTOR ‚Üí HR_DIRECTOR ‚Üí HR_OFFICER`

3. **If `directorate` = "Policy, Planning, Monitoring & Evaluation (PPME)":**
   - Standard workflow: `EMPLOYEE ‚Üí SUPERVISOR ‚Üí UNIT_HEAD ‚Üí DIRECTOR ‚Üí HR_OFFICER`

4. **If `unit` = "Internal Audit Unit":**
   - Standard workflow applies
   - Unit Head has `AUDITOR` role for read-only access to all records
   - But approval workflow still follows standard path
