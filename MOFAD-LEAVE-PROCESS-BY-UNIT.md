### ðŸ›ï¸ OFFICE OF THE MINISTER

#### 1. Ministerial Secretariat

**Employee**: Staff in Ministerial Secretariat

**Process:**
1. **Employee Submits Leave Request**
   - Fills form with all required MoFAD fields
   - System validates balance, attachments, declaration
   - Creates leave request with workflow: `SUPERVISOR â†’ UNIT_HEAD â†’ CHIEF_DIRECTOR â†’ HR_OFFICER`

2. **Level 1: SUPERVISOR Approval**
   - **Approver**: Designated supervisor within Secretariat (e.g., Senior Secretary)
   - **Action**: Reviews request, checks staffing impact
   - **Can**: Approve or Reject (with comments)
   - **Notification**: Employee notified of decision

3. **Level 2: UNIT_HEAD Approval**
   - **Approver**: Secretary to Minister (Unit Head)
   - **Action**: Reviews request, considers ministerial schedule
   - **Can**: Approve or Reject (with comments)
   - **Notification**: Employee notified

4. **Level 3: CHIEF_DIRECTOR Approval**
   - **Approver**: Chief Director
   - **Action**: Administrative oversight, ensures no conflict with ministerial activities
   - **Can**: Approve or Reject (with comments)
   - **Notification**: Employee notified

5. **Level 4: HR_OFFICER Final Approval**
   - **Approver**: HR Officer
   - **Action**: Final validation, balance deduction, record locking
   - **Can**: Approve or Reject (with comments)
   - **On Approval**: 
     - Leave balance deducted
     - Record locked
     - Approval letter generated
     - Employee notified
   - **On Rejection**: Balance not deducted, employee notified

**Total Levels**: 4  
**Estimated Time**: 3-5 working days
